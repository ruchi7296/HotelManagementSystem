What Sushi is...

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.

Nigiri

The topping can be seafood, vegetables, meat, omelet, or tofu, and in addition to fresh seafood, the fish may be pickled in soy sauce or vinegar, or broiled with a blowtorch.

Oshizushi

The toppings include fish like mackerel or gizzard shad, and may also be decoratively layered with edible leaves like bamboo
